Planting for non-planters

First, we must realize that not everyone has a green thumb.

Where to begin

You will want to gather all of the materials to start your flower garden. It is essential that you have the poper tools and gardening equipment so that your starter garden is healthy and in a position to grow. In the next section you will find a list of essential gardening equipment.

My Garden Shopping list:

These can be found at your local Home Depot or Walmart:

  • Planting Soil
  • Pots - Small and large
  • spade
  • Gardening gloves

few important things to note before testing out your green thumb:

  1. Not all flowers/plants grow in all seasons
  2. Some plants are better kept indoors instead of outdoors
  3. There are many options for seasonal flowers and plants that require minimal care
